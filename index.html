Competent Scaffolder training

A course called "competent person scaffold training" goes on the safety considerations of setting up, taking down, and inspecting several kinds of scaffolds, including frame, tube & clamp, and systems scaffolds. The rules, elements, foundations, guardrails, fall prevention, access procedures, loading restrictions, and stability of scaffolds are all covered in the course.

Defensive Driving Training

This training provides the knowledge and information necessary to drive defensively while addressing an organization's goals for safety. There are specific courses for Two wheelers riders, Light Motor Vehicle drivers and Heavy Goods vehicle drivers.

    The courses will cover the following topics.
  1. Techniques and tactics for defensive driving to lower the risk of accidents.
  2. How driving is impacted by use of drugs, alcohol, physical ailments, and emotions
  3. How to cope with aggressive, distracted, and fatigued driving
  4. Personal duty and accountability while driving.
  5. Driving in different environmental conditions and how to manage them.
  6. The risks associated with using a cell phone while driving.
  7. Driver competency and new technologies in vehicles.
  8. On-road assessment of drivers post classroom training.

Our Safety Audits

A systematic and methodical process of looking at the plant, its processes and procedures. Whether they are being followed and is meeting the requirements laid out by the management is the purpose of an audit. Our expert and experienced auditors can help validate the safety systems and identify gaps to improve safety.

Plant Safety audits

An help to sound, safe plant management is the safety audit. Plant workers will be involved in audits that examine training, job experience, procedure knowledge, emergency protocols, and other plant operating manuals. Following that, a formal report and action plan are created and maintained.

Fire safety audits

: An instrument for evaluating the fire safety requirements of a structure or organization is a fire safety audit. An extensive fire safety audit looks at the built-in fire hazards of routine activities and makes recommendations for how to lower such risks. For every type of building and industry, including hotels, hospitals, houses, schools, manufacturing facilities, chemical plants, power plants, FMCG firms, foundries, mines, etc., AGEP India performs fire safety audits.

Logistics Safety Audit:

An area of high neglect when it comes to safety is Logistics. It is a nightmare for any logistics manager to manage safety. As time passes the capacities of plants keep increasing, however the infrastructure remains the same and rarely are they augmented based on the throughput. Here a Logistics Safety audit helps identify areas of risk and suggest measures for improving the movement of vehicles or change in design of infrastructure or processes or use technology to make the operations safe.
